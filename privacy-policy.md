# Privacy Policy for Quick Prompt

Last updated: April 2026

## Overview
Quick Prompt is a Chrome Extension that runs your saved AI prompts 
on any webpage. We are committed to protecting your privacy.

## Data We Collect
We do not collect any personal data from users.

All data (saved prompts and API key) is stored locally on your 
device using Chrome's built-in storage (chrome.storage.local) 
and is never transmitted to our servers.

## How Your Data Is Used
- Saved prompts: stored locally on your device only
- API key: stored locally on your device, sent directly to 
Groq API to process your requests
- Webpage content: sent directly to Groq API when you run a 
prompt, we do not store or access this data

## Third Party Services
This extension uses the Groq API to process prompts. 
Please refer to Groq's privacy policy at https://groq.com/privacy-policy
